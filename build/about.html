About us

Together in Comfort

We love to spend time with our family, friends and colleagues. We love to travel with big groups and have fun together, explore a variety of cultures and continents, countries and cities in their beautiful diversity.

Comfort and privacy are absolute essential for our holidays. We would like to have the same life quality as at home during our travels and need to know that no one is watching us in moments of relaxation and celebrating, as well as not have to worry about the neighbors and possible mutual discomfort.

Our mission
To make people happy

Accordingly we are looking for accommodations with a lot of space and options to enjoy the time together as well as enough bedrooms for everybody, in the moments to retire. We prefer authentic houses where mode of life and the country culture are presented on the best way, as well as locations from where we reach the city center and most important places of the surrounding area as quickly as possible.

How it all started

Over time it became apparent that only few houses, usually located in the resorts or provinces are in line with our needs. And if we travelled to big metropolis or non-resort towns, the task of finding an appropriate house for all our criteria became impossible.

So we decided to create such houses for us and our guests ourselves and build the first global chain of luxury villas “VILLA EMG”: we offer the best accommodations and "home feeling" for big families and groups, as well as guests traveling with their staff (governesses, drivers, cooks, etc.) and are accustomed to a certain level of comfort all over the world.
