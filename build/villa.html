Description

This incredible half-timbered villa spans approximately 350m² and is situated in a serene and secluded location. The villa is encompassed by its very own forest, which spans approximately 2,5 hectares, and also boasts a beautiful lake complete with a jetty, jacuzzi offering unique comfort for large families or business groups up to 20 guests. Located in the charming town, between Osnabrück and Bielefeld, this villa offers aperfect
blend tranquility and urban accessibility.
The area is known for its beautiful Westphalian countryside, historic architecture, and excellent hiking trails in the nearby Wiehengebirge mountains.
The region's rich cultural heritage is reflected in the town's well-preserved half-timbered buildings and local traditions. Whether you're interested in outdoor activities, cultural experiences, or simply enjoying the peaceful countryside, provides an ideal base for exploring the best of Northern Germany.
